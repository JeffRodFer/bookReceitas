Ingredients:

  • 1 kg of beef fillet
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup of dark beer

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180ºC.
  2. Season the meat with salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan and brown the meat on all sides.
  4. Place the meat in a baking dish and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove the meat from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, in the same frying pan, sauté the onion in butter until golden brown.
  7. Add the beer and let it reduce by half.
  8. Remove the meat from the baking dish and slice it.
  9. Place the meat on a platter and pour the sauce over it.
  10. Serve with rice and vegetables.
